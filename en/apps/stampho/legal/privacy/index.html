Stampho Privacy Policy

The Service currently does not include account registration, login, personalized advertising, or behavior-based marketing as core features. We do not directly collect or store credit card numbers, bank account numbers, or other payment method details inside the app.

3. Retention Period

As a rule, we do not continuously collect or store user personal information on external servers controlled by us.

  • In-app records, categories, stamp information, original/rendered media: stored in the app's local storage on the user's device until the user deletes the record or deletes the app.
  • Support emails: deleted without delay after support is completed, unless retention is required by applicable law or dispute handling needs.

Paid app purchases are processed through Apple App Store. We do not directly retain payment method information inside the app.

4. Third-Party Disclosure

We do not sell personal information to external businesses or provide it to third parties without user consent.

The following cases may be considered exceptions.

  • When a user directly chooses an external destination, such as a specific app, messenger, SNS, or cloud service, through the iOS share sheet and shares a file
  • When Apple processes information according to its own policies during paid app purchase, refund, or receipt handling through the App Store

In these cases, the policies of Apple or the external service selected by the user may apply to the relevant processing.

5. Processing Delegation

We currently do not assume ongoing outsourcing of user personal information to external processors for the Service's core features.

6. Destruction of Personal Information

When the purpose of processing is achieved, we destroy personal information without delay.

  • When an in-app record is deleted: related local records and managed media files are deleted.
  • When the app is deleted: data stored inside the app sandbox may be removed according to operating system policy.
  • When support email data is deleted: unnecessary information is deleted after support is completed.

However, files copied to external apps or services through sharing, and transaction information retained by Apple during App Store operations, are managed under the policies of those respective parties.

7. International Transfer

We currently do not assume ongoing transfer of user personal information to overseas servers controlled by us for core Service operation.

However, overseas processing may occur in the following cases according to platform or external service policies.

  • When the user sends files to an app or service operated by an overseas provider through the iOS share sheet
  • When information is processed according to Apple platform policies during App Store distribution, payment, refund, or download operations

In these cases, the policies of the relevant platform or service provider, rather than the Company, may apply first.

8. User Rights and How to Exercise Them

Users may exercise the following rights against the Company at any time.

  1. Request access to personal information
  2. Request correction or deletion of personal information
  3. Request suspension of personal information processing
  4. Request support or complaint handling

Rights may be exercised through the contact channel below.

  • Support email: studioyona.official@gmail.com

Users may also take the following actions directly in the app.

  • Delete records
  • Edit category and stamp content
  • Organize local records other than shared files

The Service is not intended for children under 14. If we become aware that personal information of a child under 14 has been processed, we will review deletion or necessary restrictions without delay.

9. Security Measures

We apply the following measures to protect personal information.

  1. In-app records and media are stored in local device storage.
  2. The core Service is designed to operate without a member server or external analytics SDK.
  3. Unless the user chooses to share, captured results and stamp data are not transmitted externally by default.

10. Cookies, Behavioral Information, and Personalized Ads

We currently do not use cookies, advertising identifier-based tracking, personalized ads, or behavioral information collection as core Service features.
