Stampho

Stampho is made for placing useful stamps directly onto photos and videos. Capture, add up to four text stamps, manage frequently used presets by category, and come back to edit or share local records.

Key features

  • Capture photos or videos and place text stamps right away
  • Edit and reposition up to four stamps for the record you want to keep
  • Manage stamp presets by category so repeated records are faster to create
  • Keep results on your device, revisit them later, and share them when you choose
